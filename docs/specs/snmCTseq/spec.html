snmCT-seq

  • https://doi.org/10.1016/j.xgen.2022.100107
  • Single-nucleus methylcytosine and transcriptome sequencing (snmCT-seq). Specification assumes 2x151bp paired-end reads with 10bp dual indices based on snmC-seq3 and SMART-seq2. Bisulfite-converted gDNA reads (methyl) and cDNA reads (RNA) are separated in silico.
  • methyl, rna
